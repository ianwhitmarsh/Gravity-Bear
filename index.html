Quick answers

Before you choose GRAVITY BEAR.

Do I need climbing experience?

No. GRAVITY BEAR is beginner-friendly, and the whole point is to start where you are.

What is included with monthly membership?

Monthly membership includes unlimited climbing, full facility access, the fitness area, coworking space, cafe access, Tension Board 2 access, and members-only hours.

What is included in the 2-week trial?

The trial is a prepaid 2-week membership for new members. It does not auto-renew, gear rental is not included, and the initiation fee is waived if you upgrade to recurring membership.

Where is GRAVITY BEAR?

GRAVITY BEAR Bouldering Gym is at 618 E Third Street in downtown Tulsa, Oklahoma.

Can I compare every plan first?

Yes. The membership catalog includes monthly, student, annual, prepaid, family, day pass, and punch pass options.
